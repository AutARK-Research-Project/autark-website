First regulars’ table: “Neurodivergent Round” at Traumtänzer on 20.09.2023

    20. September 2023

    The Traumtänzer has always offered refuge to the most diverse individuals of the TU Dresden: there are few other places where you will find so many neurodivergent students.

    So you’ll also find many people there who have challenges in their studies. So: let’s talk about it, and let’s make it better!

    This evening, as part of Neurodivergent@TU Dresden, we would like to talk with you about your needs for your studies, what really helps you, and what has a negative impact on your academic success.

    With this regular round table, we want to offer you a safe space for mutual support and exchange in a relaxed atmosphere.
    We also want to exermine structural interventions with you to enable more accessibility at TU Dresden.
